Privacy Policy

PrivacyCam is designed to help you redact sensitive information while keeping your photo and video workflow local-first.

Media processing

PrivacyCam does not upload your photos or videos to our servers for detection, redaction, preview, or export. Your media remains on your device unless you intentionally share it using iOS sharing features or another app.

Permissions

  • Photos access is used when you import media, save redacted output, or use the share extension.
  • Camera access is used only when you capture a photo in the app.
  • Local storage may be used temporarily while processing media and preparing exports.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details. The app may check purchase status to unlock PrivacyCam Pro features.

Support

If you contact support, we may receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address, device model, iOS version, screenshots, or issue description. Do not send private photos or videos unless you intentionally choose to share them for troubleshooting.

Children's privacy

PrivacyCam is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The latest version will be available on this page.
